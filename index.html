FAQ

Questions retailers usually ask.

Is there a minimum order quantity?
No. Test with a single carton — or a single item. Most clients start small, confirm the quality and process, then scale up.
How do payments work?
After you approve the quote, we send a formal invoice payable by bank wire, Wise, or Payoneer. We purchase only after payment clears — and you've already seen every cost line before committing.
What if an item arrives damaged or wrong?
Every item is inspected and photographed before shipping, which prevents most issues. If something is our mistake, we refund or replace it — simple as that.
What products can you source?
Almost anything legally exportable from Japan: stationery, kitchenware, tools, cosmetics, toys, collectibles, snacks, craft supplies and more. We'll flag anything with export restrictions (e.g. certain foods, batteries) in the quote.
How long does shipping take?
Typically 3–7 days by express courier (DHL/FedEx) or 1–3 weeks by economy options. Your quote includes both so you can choose.
Can you find products I only have a photo of?
Yes — that's most of our job. Send a photo or rough description and we'll identify the product, the maker, and the best price available in Japan.
